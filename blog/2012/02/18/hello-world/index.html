Hello World

Feb 18th, 2012

Have you ever found yourself in the situation where you we’re assigned a task or given a problem you never had to solve before, i.e. a strange bug you’ve never seen or some code of yours not behaving like expected? Did you google it up, ask a friend or co-worker for help or maybe grabbed that programming book you read a while ago to look for a solution? Either way you’ll find a solution sooner or later and you’ll fix the problem and continue happily with your duty. But you know what? It won’t be the last time you’ve been in this situation. The same bug/strange behaviour will occur again, maybe months later. Will you remember how you solved it the last time? Well, if you had kept a solution log you could easily look it up.

So here it is, my public solution log, my “notes to myself”. By making it publicly available it even may be useful to other people.

Now the hard part is the writing. Until now I jotted down some notes in various text files on my computer — unstructured and incomplete howtos and code snippets. I’ll have to group, review and rewrite them so they can be published here. I hope I finally found a suitable platform by deciding to ride with Octopress. We will see.
